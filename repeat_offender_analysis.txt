     - The HIERARCHY of planets matters: double power of a malefic (Saturn-Saturday)
       is different from double power of a benefic (Jupiter-Thursday)
     - IMPLICATION: Double power SHORT makes sense for Saturn/Mars days but NOT for
       Jupiter/Venus/Sun days. Filter by WHICH planet is doubled.

     - The EMA200 trend and volume conditions serve as "radicality checks"
     - IMPLICATION: The EMA200 filter IS the astrological equivalent of checking
       whether the chart is radical
